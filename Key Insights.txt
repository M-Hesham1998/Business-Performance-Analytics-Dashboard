KEY INSIGHTS

Revenue & Sales
- Revenue grew steadily over time, increasing from approximately $585K per month in early 2015 to more than $1.8M by mid-2017.
- The business experienced a slowdown during the first half of 2016, but sales recovered strongly in the second half of the year.
- May and June recorded the highest revenue and profit, indicating stronger customer demand during these months.
- Wednesday was the best-performing sales day of the week.

Profit Analysis
- The business maintained a strong Profit Margin of 41.97%, reflecting healthy profitability.
- Bikes were the primary driver of profit, contributing the largest share of total profit.
- Road Bikes and Mountain Bikes generated the highest profit among all product categories.
- Tires & Tubes received a high number of orders but produced relatively low profit compared to other categories.
- Mountain-200 was the most profitable product.

Customer Insights
- Customers with a Bachelor's Degree generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Middle-Income customers contributed more revenue and profit than High-Income customers.
- Customers in Professional occupations generated the highest profit.
- Maurice was the most profitable customer during the analysis period.

Products & Returns
- Water Bottle - 30 oz. recorded the highest number of returns among all products.
- Out of 293 products, 130 products had no sales, indicating opportunities to improve inventory performance.
- The overall Return Rate was only 2.17%, showing that most products performed well.

Regional Performance
- The United States was the strongest-performing market overall.
- Canada generated the lowest profit among the major countries in the dataset.
- The Central region in the United States was the weakest-performing area, generating only $3.1K in revenue from 8 customers and 9 orders.

Time Intelligence
- Revenue increased consistently year over year throughout the analysis period.
- Performance in 2017 was significantly stronger than the same periods in previous years.
- A clear business recovery began in the second half of 2016, where growth shifted from negative results to strong positive performance.
- Overall YoY Growth reached 166.24%, demonstrating substantial business expansion.


BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

- Increase marketing efforts during May and June to maximize revenue during peak demand periods.
- Prioritize Road Bikes and Mountain Bikes, as they generate the highest profit.
- Investigate the high return volume of Water Bottle - 30 oz. to reduce return-related costs.
- Review the 130 unsold products and consider promotions, bundling strategies, or discontinuation.
- Focus on Middle-Income customers, as they represent the most valuable customer segment.
- Analyze the weak performance of the Central region and identify opportunities to increase sales, customer acquisition, and market reach.
